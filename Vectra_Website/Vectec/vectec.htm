VECTEC® LAUNDRY ADDITIVE --NOT AVAILABLE IN STORES

PURPOSE OF VECTEC®:
VECTEC® IS A LIQUID LAUNDRY ADDITIVE THAT CAN BE ADDED TO THE FINAL RINSE CYCLE

- add 3 oz. per 8lbs of laundry in the initial cycle.

- 2 oz. per 25 lbs. thereafter

VECTEC® is to be used on cottons and linens. VECTEC® will insure positive results and give the best protection for the extended life on all your washable work clothes, uniforms and washable children's clothing, bed linens, and sheets.

BENEFITS OF VECTEC®:

1. Enhances the release of stains and soil on subsequent
laundry cycles without product buildup.

2. 8-10% faster drying time for laundry since water
extraction time is shortened.

3. Much shorter pressing time with 15-20% savings on hard
to finish cottons.

4. Increases ease of ironing by 30% with a significant
reduction of surface friction.

5. Strengthens fibers and thereby enhances the durability
of the fabric.

6. Acts as a permanent finish to the fabric thereby serving
as an alternative to sizing and starch and fabric softener
